You ask, We tell

    Do I need to subscribe every month?

    Ours is a subscription based model. You get toys delivered for as many months as you pay. We recommend longer subscriptions as you will enjoy getting variety of toys for your kids.

    Can the subscription be cancelled?

    Of course you can, before the package for that month has been delivered. Whatever balance remaining in your account, will be transferred back to you. But please do mail us, as to what happened? We will try our best to improvise or solve it.

    Can i return or exchange a package if my kid already has those toys?

    Sorry! this is not possible. Please understand that from our point of view, every kid will have some toys, and hence there is a good chance that one or two months subscriptions in a year would be the same as what you already own. It will not be practical for us to take care of this with everybody. But as you continue our subscription, we are sure you will be more than happy with what we provide and the occurrence of such scenarios will be minimal.

    I can instead buy toys online. How are you different?

    We provide toys that are appropriate for your child's age delivered to your home. Each variety of toy instills different skills in kids, and with our subscriptions, we try to bring in all the necessary skills for your child's developmental milestones. Usually, kids and parents end up buying similar kind of toys as per the child's liking. This is definitely not wrong, but with our surprise boxes delivered at home, kids start engaging in new toys and learn new skills.And we also provide unique learning kits that are not regularly available in market and also make your child "academically ready" and creative.
